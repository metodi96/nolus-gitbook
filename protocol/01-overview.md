# Overview

_Source: https://hub.nolus.io/en/articles/9680214-overview_

**Nolus introduces a new DeFi primitive through its asset-backed margin product—reshaping how users access leverage with a focus on capital efficiency, predictability, and risk control.**

The protocol is built on three core pillars: **fixed interest rates** that offer cost clarity and protection against market volatility, **structured liquidity** to ensure stable and reliable access to capital, and **controlled leverage** that empowers users to amplify their exposure while maintaining long-term position health.

To further safeguard users, Nolus features a **partial liquidation engine** designed to mitigate downside risk. This mechanism helps shield positions from full collateral loss, offering a layer of protection during periods of heightened market volatility and preserving user capital in uncertain conditions.

Unlike traditional margin products, **positions on Nolus are backed by the actual underlying asset**, offering increased transparency, reduced counterparty risk, and tighter alignment between the user’s position and the asset’s real market performance. This structure not only enhances trust but also unlocks access to curated, yield-bearing strategies—enabling users to put their assets to work even while leveraged.

**The protocol operates on a semi-permissioned proof-of-stake blockchain** built with the Cosmos SDK and powered by a WASM-based smart contract engine. This infrastructure brings together performance, flexibility, and security—ensuring that Nolus can scale and evolve alongside the broader Web3 ecosystem.

At the core of Nolus’ architecture is a strong focus on **interoperability**. Leveraging **IBC (Inter-Blockchain Communication)** and **Interchain Accounts**, the protocol seamlessly connects to a range of liquidity hubs without fragmenting user assets. This cross-chain integration broadens access to capital and enhances the overall utility of the platform, positioning Nolus as a gateway to a more unified and efficient DeFi experience across ecosystems
